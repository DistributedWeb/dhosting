About dHosting

dHosting is a public peer for files published with the dWeb protocol. We keep your files online while your computer is off.

dPack is a peer-to-peer repository for sharing datasets and files. When you use dPack, your files are distributed across the network of peers who you've shared your files with.

Why use dHosting? Reliability and peer revelation!

Publishing with dPack means that peers will contribute bandwidth, but only if they're online and sharing your files. If nobody's hosting your files, then they won't be accessible.

That's where dHosting comes in. We act as a "super peer" that makes sure your content is always available.
