Features

Reliable rehosting

dHosting acts as a "super peer" and rehosts your dPack vaults, so your files are always available, even when you're offline.

Vault history backup

dPack maintains a log of all the changes you make to your vault's files. In addition to storing your vault's files, we store a backup of the entire history log.

HTTPS mirroring

In addition to rehosting your files on the dWeb peer-to-peer network, we mirror the content on the Web so you can share it with anyone.

DNS shortnames

Pick a custom shortname for each of your vaults, like test.jared.dhosting.io.
